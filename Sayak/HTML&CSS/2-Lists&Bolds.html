India is home to several cities renowned for their historical monuments. Delhi boasts the Red Fort and Qutub Minar, while Agra is famous for the Taj Mahal. Jaipur showcases Amer Fort and Hawa Mahal, while Hyderabad offers the Charminar. Other notable cities include Varanasi, Mysore, and Khajuraho, each rich in heritage.
